## Bundling [Beginner]

### Why is bundling better than serving modules directly?

* ⚗️ not all browsers *support modules*
* 🚈 *fewer requests*
* ✂️ *better gzipping*
* 📧 *less* browser *overhead*

### What's worse when bundling?

* 💸 Inefficient *caching*
